Description :

Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful and aromatic dish that brings together tender chicken pieces in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of traditional Indian spices. To prepare this dish, boneless chicken pieces are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices like cumin, coriander, garam masala, and turmeric, allowing the flavors to penetrate deeply.

After marination, the chicken is slow-cooked with diced onions, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes in a crockpot or slow cooker, allowing the ingredients to meld together over several hours. The slow cooking process ensures that the chicken becomes incredibly tender and absorbs all the delicious flavors of the sauce. Towards the end of the cooking time, cream or coconut milk is added to create a luscious, creamy texture, balancing out the spices and adding a hint of sweetness.

Ingredients :

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Plain yogurt, for marinating the chicken
  • Spices for the chicken marinade, such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, and paprika
  • Diced onions
  • Minced Garlic etc

Steps :

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a mixing bowl, combine the chicken pieces with plain yogurt and a mixture of spices such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, and paprika. Ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
  2. Prepare the Slow Cooker: Lightly grease the insert of your slow cooker to prevent sticking. You can also use a slow cooker liner for easier cleanup.
  3. SautÃ© Aromatics: In a skillet or frying pan, heat a bit of oil over medium heat. Add diced onions, minced garlic, and minced ginger. SautÃ© until the onions are translucent and the mixture is fragrant, about 3-5 minutes.

  5. Season and Cook: Sprinkle additional spices over the chicken and tomato mixture, such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, paprika, and chili powder for extra heat if desired. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir everything together gently to combine.
  6. Slow Cook: Cover the slow cooker with its lid and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Cooking times may vary depending on your slow cooker model and the size of the chicken pieces.
  7. Add Cream or Coconut Milk:Once the chicken is cooked, pour in heavy cream or coconut milk to add creaminess to the sauce. Stir well to incorporate the cream into the sauce.
  8. Adjust Seasoning:Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed, adding more salt, pepper, or spices according to your preference.
  9. Serve: Serve the Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala hot over steamed basmati rice or with warm naan bread. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves for a burst of freshness.

Enjoy your homemade Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala with its rich, flavorful sauce and tender chicken pieces!
